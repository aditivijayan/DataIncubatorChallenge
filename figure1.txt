This Figure shows the rating distribution of the most rated item in the data set. 
The bars show the fraction of the total rating given by all the users. 
This is a highly popular item as more than half the people who read this item gave it a 
five star rating. Increasingly lower fractions of user rated it poorly. 
It is interesting to note that nearly twice as many people gave a one-star rating 
as compared to a two-star rating, indicating the earlier mentioned bias of 
strong emotions driving strong actions.
